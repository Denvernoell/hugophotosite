Welcome!

This site is dedicated to Jim Martin, and includes over 10,000 photos he took over a period of 80 years, as he wanted to share them with the public works industry.

The archived photos were in the form of slides, organized in sheets of 20 and files inside 18 boxes. Each slide included a handwritten description, as well as a numbering system, both of which were preserved in the name of the scanned file. The photos are organized by decade, starting in the 1910s, and ending in the 1990s.

Thanks to Akel Engineering Group for scanning and preserving these photos, and for developing this web site.a
