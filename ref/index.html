About

Why

These photos were taken by James Martin. James Martin was very involved in public works and had a love for photography and what it brought. These photos were scanned because of the lasting impact that James Martin had on the public works industry as well as those in the community here in Fresno.

How

With 18 boxes of photos and a vison in mind, we developed a process that allowed for the scanning of over 10,000 images from a range of approximately 80 years.

Each box contained various amounts of photos, each of which had a story to tell. The photos are on slides which are contained in sheets of 20 photos. These photos were scanned individually with photo scanners and named according to the writing on the borders of each photo.
